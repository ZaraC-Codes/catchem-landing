About

What Is CatchEm?

CatchEm is a 2D pixel-art Pokemon catching game built on the blockchain. Explore an overworld map, find wild Pokemon hiding in rustling grass, throw PokeBalls to catch them, and collect unique NFT trading cards when you succeed. Every throw is verified on-chain with provable randomness. Every catch earns you a real NFT.

Gameplay

How It Works

1

Buy PokeBalls

Purchase PokeBalls using SOL, APE, or USDC. Four tiers available — from the humble Poke Ball ($1) to the legendary Master Ball ($49.90).

2

Explore the Map

Move your character around the pixel-art overworld using arrow keys, WASD, or tap-to-move on mobile. Look for rustling grass patches.

3

Choose Your Mode

Adventure Mode: Limited attempts — Pokemon relocate after 3 misses. Encounter Mode: Unlimited throws for relaxed gameplay.

4

Throw & Catch

Walk up to a wild Pokemon and throw your ball. Catch results use verifiable on-chain randomness. Higher-tier balls = better odds.

5

Collect Your NFT

Successful catches award a random Pokemon NFT card. Each card has unique art, rarity, edition, and series attributes.
